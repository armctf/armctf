armctf is a project that provides downloadable vulnerable virtual machines, rebuilt from amd64 to arm64. it's for users to practice ctf challenges on arm-based systems. vulnerable machines are sourced from vulnhub, hackmyvm and vulnyx. that's why full credit goes to the original creators. for extended information and contributing, please read readme.
